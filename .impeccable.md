## Design Context

### Users
PetCare+ is used by pet owners, veterinarians, and administrators who need quick access to pet medical records, vaccination schedules, insurance policies, and reminders during everyday care and clinic operations.

### Brand Personality
Trustworthy, calm, and operational. The interface should feel like a reliable medical workspace rather than a marketing site.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light, clinical, and data-forward with restrained teal and warm accent colors. Prioritize dense but readable tables, timelines, status badges, and route-level dashboards over decorative hero sections.

### Design Principles
- Keep health and policy status scannable at a glance.
- Use stable layouts that support repeated daily workflows.
- Surface overdue and renewal risks without visual noise.
- Keep RBAC and notification state visible but unobtrusive.
- Prefer precise clinical copy and compact controls.
